Girls in ICT Day

International Girls in ICT Day is celebrated on the fourth Thursday in April to stress the importance of girls in I.C.T. and encourage more girls and young women to pursue STEM education. This year, it takes place on April 25. The theme for this years Girls ICT Day is "Leadership"

DATE : 25th April 2024

TIME : 7.00PM - 8.00PM

Guest Speaker

Julia Suzuki is a purpose-driven Data & AI professional with an entrepreneurial spirit who specializes in Data & AI for Well-Being. She has consulting experience for 14 years with Accenture, helping its clients transform into intelligent enterprises. She met Njogu through KaggleX BIPOC program where she served as the talent advisor and mentor for the cohort 3 of the program. She is originally from Japan (she grew up in a rural area called Otsuhata in Tochigi-prefecture, Japan) and now live in Michigan, USA with her husband, and a cat. She loves to eat, dance, play (with her cat,) and read, learn, & do with data & AI and the science of well-being.
